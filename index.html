  • New School Canteen

    The ideal school canteen is one that caters to the broadest possible clientele – taking into account if their patrons identify as vegetarian, vegan, kosher, halal, coeliac, lactose intolerant, have a nut allergy, or have no such dietary requirements.They are prepared in very hygienic surroundings by our school cook, and tables to sit on and eat, and ovens to keep the food warm.   • Implemented Online Attendance Management System

    1. In most people’s lives, education is the most important component. Nowadays, online Study or Learning turns out to be more and more practiced but It has some pros and cons too. 2. Online education aids in the solution of the time problem. It is a great alternative to traditional universities, especially for people who can’t afford the time and money. 3. Furthermore, distant learning is less expensive and, for some people, the most effective technique of learning new material. 4.Students may also learn on their own time and at their own pace through online learning. It takes much less time than face-to-face learning. 5. Online education allows anybody, regardless of nationality or place of residence, to get a diploma or degree from any university anywhere in the globe. 6. Lack of personal contact between teacher and student is less efficient and also there is a lack of communication with fellow students to share their experiences. 7. Student competition may be quite motivating, and pupils can only learn from it. 8. Online education usually is only in writing. It might be difficult for certain persons to articulate their knowledge in words, which might delay information absorption. 9. Online learning would be a great tool to learn the theory-based topics For Example side of football, like its history and the rules of the game. However, it cannot be useful to transfer practical knowledge like how to play the game. 10. Even the top online class cannot completely replace face-to-face interaction with an educator, thus typical classrooms should not, in my opinion, be substituted by online learning.
